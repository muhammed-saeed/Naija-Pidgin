Delaaya, Tobaya and Nekoda shidren-shidren and dem bi 652.
Dis na some among di priests family wey dem nor fit show sey dem bi Israel pipol: Hobaaya, Hakkoz, Barzillai (wey marry one of Barzillai dotas, wey bi Gilead pesin and e kon dey ansa en fada-in-law name.)
Since dem nor fit prove who dia grand-grand papa bi, dem nor kon asept dem as priest.
So di govnor tell dem sey dem nor go fit chop di food wey dem offa give God, until di land don get priest wey go use Urim and Tummim.
Di total pipol wey kom back from Babilon na 42,360.
Even doh dem nor kount di men and wimen savant, dem still bi 7,337 wey kom back. Di men and wimen muzicians na 200.
Horse na 736, melu na 245,
Kamels na 435 and donkey na 6,720.
Wen di pipol rish God Temple for Jerusalem, some among di leaders for each family drop ofrin from dia mind make dem join am take ribuild God Temple for Jerusalem.
Dem give di amount wey dem fit give for di work and di total na: 61,000 pounds for gold, 5,000 pounds for silva and 100 garment for di priests.
Di priests, Levi pipol and some of di pipol, di singas, di temple guards and di temple workers stay for di town wey dey near Jerusalem; while di remainin Israel pipol stay for di towns wey dia grand-grand papa stay before.
