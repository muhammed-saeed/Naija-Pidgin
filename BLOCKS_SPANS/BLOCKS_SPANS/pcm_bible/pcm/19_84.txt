Asaf Song
My God, make Yu nor kwayet just like dat! Make Yu nor rijet us! Nor just do like sey e nor koncern Yu!
Yu nor si di nois wey yor enemies dey make? Yu nor dey si sey yor sturbon enemies dey make mout anyhow?
Dem dey plan against yor pipol and dem won wound di ones wey Yu love.
Dem dey sey, “Make una kom make wi kill evribody for Israel finish and nobody go remember dem again.”
All of dem gada togeda and dem dey plan against Yu.
Dem wey gada so na: Edom and Ishmael pipol, Moab and Hagrit pipol;
Gebal, Ammon and Amalek pipol with doz from Filistia and Tyre.
Assyria pipol don join dem too with Lot shidren-shidren.
So, make Yu do dem wetin Yu do Midian pipol and just as Yu do Sisera and Jabin for Kishon River, na so too Yu go do dem.
Dem die for Endor and dia body kon make di grass grow well.
Make di mighty ones among dem die like Oreb and Zeeb. Make all di prince die like Zebah and Zalmunna
wey bin sey, “Make wi take dis land wey bi God own!”
My God, make Yu skata dem like leaf and san-san wey breeze dey blow!
As fire dey burn forest and smoke dey make mountin burn,
na so too Yu go take porshu dem with yor strong tonda kon make dem fear.
My God, make Yu disgrace dem finish until dem bigin praiz yor name.
Make shame and fear follow dem forever and make dem die with disgrace.
Den dem go know sey na only Yu bi God, di Pesin wey get pawa pass for di whole world.
