Wen Israel pipol dey for Shittim, dem kon dey sleep with Moab wimen.
Dis wimen invite di pipol kom dia juju festival; den di pipol chop and woship dia juju.
Bikos Israel pipol join dem woship Baal-Peor, God kon dey vex well-well for dem.
God tell Moses, “Arrest all di leaders kon hang dem before di Oga for aftanoon, so dat mi God nor go vex for Israel pipol again.”
So Moses tell di judge dem wey dey Israel sey, “Each of una must kill doz men among una wey dey woship Baal-Peor.”
At wons, one Israel man bring one Midian woman enter di tent where Moses and di whole kommunity for dey kry.
Wen Finehas wey Eleazar born, Aaron di priest grand- pikin si am, e komot from where all di pipol gada, take spear
follow di Israel man enter en tent, den kill di two of dem. So di wahala wey dey distroy Israel pipol kon stop.
Doz wey die for di wahala na 24,000.
God tell Moses:
“Finehas wey Eleazar born, wey bi Aaron di priest grand-pikin don remove my vexnashon from Israel pipol, wen e do dat tin wey e do and naim make mi nor distroy dem wen I dey vex.
So make yu tell am sey: ‘I go gi-am my kovenant wey dey bring peace.
Na en get am and en shidren go bi priest forever, bikos e take en God sirious kon make mi forgive dis pipol.’ ”
Di Israel man wey dem kill with dat Midian woman, na Zimri wey Salu born, wey bi leader for Simeon tribe.
Di Midian woman wey dem kill, na Cozbi wey Zur born. Zur na leader for Midian tribe.
Den God kommand Moses,
“Make yu attack and distroy Midian pipol,
bikos dem bring wahala to una wen dem deceive una koncerning Peor and bikos of Cozbi, wey die for di time wey Peor bring wahala enter una land.”
