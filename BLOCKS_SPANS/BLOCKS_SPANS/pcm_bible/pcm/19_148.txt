Make wi praiz God! Si as e dey good to sing praiz God! Yes, na to praiz God bi di rite tin to do!
God dey ribuild Jerusalem and E go bring en pipol kom back to Israel from di land where dem dey.
E heal pipol wey dia heart don break kon trit dia sore.
E dey kount and koll di stars by dia names.
Si as awa Oga God big rish! En pawa dey wonderful! Nobody fit undastand how God take dey know tins!
God dey support pipol wey nor dey karry body up, but E dey kill wiked pipol trowey.
So, make una sing tank God; make una use harp take sing praiz awa God.
E kover di heavens with kloud kon make rain fall for di eart, den E make grass grow on-top mountins.
E dey give food to wild animals kon feed di yong birds wen dem dey kry.
E nor kare about di pawa wey horse get or di one wey human being get.
No! But God dey happy for pipol wey dey fear and obey am; doz wey put dia hope for en love wey nor dey fail.
So, Jerusalem pipol! Make una praiz God! Pipol for Zion, make una gi-am all di praiz!
Bikos E don make di iron for una gate strong kon bless all una shidren.
E send peace kom una land kon make una chop betta food.
E send en laws kom di world; so make una si as en word kwik kom meet una!
E send snow kom like as wool dey fall; E skata snow for groun like ashes.
E trow big-big stones with fire kom di eart. So, na who fit stand face God kold breeze wey fit turn pesin to ice-blok?
But as E kommand, di kold breeze and ice-blok kon melt. E send en breeze and di ice-blok kon turn wota.
E don show Jakob en word, kommands and rules for Israel pipol.
E neva do like dis for any oda nashon; no oda nashon know en rules and kommand. So make wi praiz God!
