David Song
I go look up to di mountin! Where my help from dey kom?
My help dey kom from God, di Pesin wey make heaven and eart!
E nor go let mi kick stone fall; di Pesin wey dey guide mi nor dey sleep.
True-true, di Pesin wey dey guide Israel nor dey sleep at-all at-all.
Na God ensef dey wosh ova mi! E stand near mi kon protet mi.
Di sun nor fit do mi anytin for day time or di moon for nite.
God dey keep mi from my enemies and E dey protet my life.
God dey always protet mi both wen I go out and wen I dey kom back! E go protet mi naw and forever.
